Refund Policy

Policy on refund and cancellation/transfer of contribution received for the Art of Living programs:

  1. Due to any unforeseen circumstances, if the program stands cancelled or rescheduled, the following options are available to the registering participant:
    1. The participant may request for a transfer of registration to another similar Art of Living program to be held with a period of 90 days of earlier registration, by writing to transaction.support@in.artofliving.org
